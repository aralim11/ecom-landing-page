SET-UP IS AS EASY AS 1-2-2.5

Get in bed quick and easy with our roll-pack boxes.

Our mattress comes compressed, folded and rolled into one small box, with no parts to assemble and no off-gassing odor.

Just open the box, carefully cut the wrapping sleeve, place the bed on your frame, and you're done! Your WinkBed will magically expand right in front of you.

The only way this could be any easier is if we literally teleported it onto your frame. We're working on it, but the technology's just not there yet.
